# Terms, Privacy & Compliance

## 1. Terms of Service

### Overview
HabitaPlot™ provides a marketplace for property listings, agent services, and premium upgrades. Users must comply with platform rules and local regulations.

### Key Sections
- **Acceptance**: Users agree to platform terms by registering or using services.
- **Accounts**: Users are responsible for maintaining accurate profile and contact information.
- **Listings**: Agents and sellers must provide truthful property details and district information.
- **Payment**: Payment through MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, or card requires valid payment credentials.
- **Prohibited Content**: No fraudulent, illegal, or misleading listings.
- **Termination**: HabitaPlot reserves the right to suspend or remove accounts, listings, or payments.
- **Liability**: The platform is a marketplace, not a broker, and disclaims responsibility for third-party transactions.

## 2. Privacy Policy

### Data Collection
- **User profile**: name, email, phone, role
- **Listing data**: title, location, district, price, images
- **Payment metadata**: provider, phone number, transaction status
- **Usage data**: device, browser, IP address, search queries

### Use of Data
- Provide marketplace services and support
- Personalize search and recommendations
- Verify users and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations and payment provider requirements

### Cookies & Tracking
- Session cookies for authentication
- Analytics cookies to monitor usage and performance
- Optional marketing cookies for product updates

### Data Rights
- Users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data.
- Users can withdraw consent to marketing communications.
- Data retention follows business needs and legal requirements.

### Security
- Platform protects user data with encryption and secure authentication.
- Sensitive payment data is not stored directly and relies on payment provider tokens.
- Regular security audits and privacy reviews are recommended.

## 3. Compliance

### Local Regulations
- Uganda-specific payment provider requirements for MTN MoMo and Airtel Money.
- Ensure listings and payments comply with local anti-fraud and consumer protection laws.
- Support data protection best practices equivalent to GDPR where possible.

### Payment Compliance
- Do not store raw mobile money PINs or credentials.
- Store only transaction metadata, status, and provider references.
- Use secure environment variables for payment API keys.

### Legal Review
- This draft is intended as a product-level compliance starting point.
- Final terms and privacy policies should be reviewed by legal counsel before publication.

## 4. Implementation Notes
- Place Terms and Privacy links in footer and signup flows.
- Include clear consent checkboxes for new users.
- Maintain an audit log of terms acceptance and policy updates.
