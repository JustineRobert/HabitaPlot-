## Getting Started as an Agent {#getting-started}

### Agent Account Setup

**Registration:**

1. Go to HabitaPlot™ and click **"Sign Up"**
2. Select **"Real Estate Agent"** as your user type
3. Enter your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password
4. Choose your company (or stay independent)
5. Read and accept Terms & Privacy Policy
6. Click **"Create Account"**

### Verification & Licensing

After registration, complete verification to unlock full features:

**Step 1: Email Verification**
- Check your email for verification link
- Click link to confirm ownership
- Account becomes active

**Step 2: KYC Verification**
1. Navigate to **Account Settings** → **KYC Verification**
2. Select document type:
   - Driver's License
   - National ID
   - Passport
3. Upload clear front and back photos
4. Submit for review (24-48 hours)
5. Receive approval email when verified

**Step 3: Professional License Verification** (Optional but Recommended)
1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Professional License**
2. Upload:
   - Real estate license copy
   - License verification number
   - License state/region
3. Verification completes within 1-2 business days
4. "Licensed Agent" badge appears on your profile
   - Increases buyer trust
   - Featured in search results

**Step 4: Agency Affiliation** (Optional)
1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Agency**
2. Enter agency details or select from existing agencies
3. Your agency verification accelerates license verification
4. Your listings show agency branding

### Setting Up Your Profile

**Profile Optimization:**

1. Go to **My Profile**
2. Complete all sections:
   - Professional photo (headshot)
   - Bio/About (3-5 sentences about experience)
   - Phone number (for direct contact by clients)
   - Specializations (neighborhoods, property types)
   - Languages spoken
   - Years in industry
   - Service areas (cities/regions you serve)
   - Website (link to your personal or agency site)

**Profile Tips:**
- ✓ Use professional headshot
- ✓ Highlight your expertise and years of experience
- ✓ List specific neighborhoods you know well
- ✓ Be transparent about services and fees
- ✗ Don't use generic descriptions
- ✗ Don't make unrealistic promises

## Listing Management {#listing-management}

### Creating Your First Listing

**Getting Started:**

1. Click **"Post Listing"** (from dashboard or navigation)
2. Select property type and action:
   - Property Type: House, Apartment, Commercial, Land
   - Action: For Sale, For Rent, For Lease
3. Begin entering property details

### Step-by-Step Listing Creation

**Section 1: Property Location**

1. Enter complete address
2. City and ZIP/postal code auto-populate
3. Map pin shows exact location (adjust if needed)
4. Address appears in multiple search filters
5. Verify location accuracy (critical for buyers)

**Section 2: Property Details**

- **Total Area**: Square footage or square meters
- **Lot Size**: (If applicable, for land/houses)
- **Bedrooms**: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5+
- **Bathrooms**: Full and half baths
- **Stories/Floors**: (For buildings)
- **Year Built**: Construction year
- **Property Condition**: Excellent, Good, Fair, Needs Renovation, Under Construction
- **Furnishing**: Furnished, Unfurnished, Semi-furnished (rentals)
- **Ownership**: Freehold, Leasehold, etc.

**Section 3: Pricing**

**For Sales:**
- Asking Price
- Price Negotiable? (Yes/No)
- Reason for sale (optional)

**For Rentals:**
- Monthly Rent
- Security Deposit
- Additional Fees (parking, utilities, etc.)
- Lease Term: 6 months, 1 year, 2 years, Flexible
- Minimum Lease Period (if applicable)

**Section 4: Description**

Write a compelling property description (300-1000 characters):

**What to Include:**
- ✓ Unique selling points (newly renovated, ocean view, etc.)
- ✓ Condition details (excellent condition, move-in ready)
- ✓ Recent improvements or renovations
- ✓ Key features (parking, garden, premium location)
- ✓ Perfect for... (families, investors, professionals)

**What to Avoid:**
- ✗ Unrealistic claims ("mansion," "palace")
- ✗ Vague descriptions without details
- ✗ Offensive or discriminatory language
- ✗ Contact info directly (use platform messaging)
- ✗ All CAPS or excessive punctuation

**Example Good Description:**
> "Stunning 3-bedroom home in prime neighborhood. Recently renovated with modern kitchen, hardwood floors throughout. Private backyard with mature trees. Walking distance to top-rated schools and parks. Perfect family home!"

**Section 5: Amenities**

Check all applicable amenities (buyers filter by these):
- Air Conditioning / Heating
- Parking (1, 2, 3+ spots)
- Garage (Attached, Detached, Carport)
- Garden / Yard
- Balcony / Patio / Terrace
- Swimming Pool
- Gym / Fitness Center
- Elevator
- Security System
- Pet-Friendly
- Furnished
- Laundry (In-unit, Building)
- Storage
- Wheelchair Accessible

### Photo & Video Upload

**Photo Guidelines:**

Upload 15-25 high-quality photos in this order:
1. **Exterior** (front, side, back)
2. **Main Living Areas** (living room, dining room)
3. **Kitchen** (full view, counter detail)
4. **Master Bedroom** (full room, detail)
5. **Other Bedrooms** (individual photos)
6. **Bathrooms** (one per bathroom)
7. **Special Features** (garden, pool, garage)
8. **Neighborhood** (street view, nearby amenities)

**Photo Quality Tips:**
- ✓ Use natural lighting (daytime, sunny day)
- ✓ Keep photos straight/horizontal
- ✓ Use wide-angle lens to show space
- ✓ Show room condition clearly
- ✓ Declutter and clean spaces before photo
- ✓ Stage photos (not overly decorated)
- ✓ Show outdoor spaces and landscaping
- ✗ Blurry or low-resolution photos
- ✗ Dark, shadowy photos
- ✗ Extreme close-ups or weird angles
- ✗ Photos of people (privacy)
- ✗ Watermarks or competitor logos

**Cover Photo:**
- The first photo shown in search results
- Should be the best exterior or most attractive room view
- Drag to reorder: most important photos first

**Video Tour:**
- Optional but highly effective
- 2-5 minute walkthrough
- Show flow of home, key features
- Start outside, walk through systematically
- Professional quality recommended

**Uploading:**
1. Click **"Upload Photos"**
2. Drag and drop or click to select
3. Multiple selection allowed (10-25 at once)
4. Arrange order by dragging
5. Add captions to photos (optional but helpful)
6. Mark cover photo
7. Upload video (if available)

### Listing Preview & Publishing

**Preview Your Listing:**

1. Click **"Preview Listing"**
2. See exactly what buyers will see
3. Check:
   - All photos display correctly
   - Description is clear
   - Price and details accurate
   - No spelling or grammar errors
   - Photos are in good order

**Publish Your Listing:**

1. After preview, click **"Publish Listing"**
2. Listing becomes immediately visible to all users
3. Begins appearing in search results
4. Send to pending admin review (24-48 hours)
5. Once approved, becomes fully featured

**After Publishing:**
- Receive confirmation email
- See listing in "My Listings"
- Track views and inquiries
- Begin receiving buyer/renter contacts

### Managing Your Listings

**View Your Listings:**

1. Go to **Manage Listings** or **"My Listings"**
2. See all your listings with:
   - Thumbnail photos
   - Price
   - Status (Active, Pending, Approved, Deactivated)
   - Posted date
   - Views & Inquiries count
   - Actions buttons

**Listing Status Meanings:**

- **Draft**: Unsaved, not published
- **Pending**: Submitted for admin review
- **Active**: Published and visible in searches
- **Featured**: Promoted (appears at top)
- **Deactivated**: Hidden from search (can reactivate)
- **Sold/Rented**: Marked as completed transaction

**Editing a Listing:**

1. Click on listing or **"Edit"** button
2. Update any information:
   - Price
   - Description
   - Amenities
   - Photos
   - Any details
3. Changes take effect immediately
4. Older listings with recent edits bump up in search rank

**Deactivating a Listing:**

1. Click listing
2. Select **"Deactivate"**
3. Reason (optional):
   - Property sold/rented
   - Temporarily not showing
   - Other
4. Listing goes hidden but can be reactivated
5. If actually sold, mark as "Sold" to show transaction completed

**Deleting a Listing:**

1. Click listing
2. Select **"Delete"**
3. **Warning**: Cannot be undone
4. Use only if you want to completely remove the listing
5. Better practice: Deactivate instead (keeps history)

### Bulk Listing Upload

*Premium feature for uploading many listings at once*

1. Go to **Tools** → **Bulk Upload**
2. Download the Excel template
3. Fill in property details (one row per property):
   - Address *(required)*
   - Bedrooms, Bathrooms
   - Price
   - Description
   - Amenities (comma-separated)
4. Upload completed file
5. System validates data
6. Review and confirm
7. All listings upload at once

**Bulk Upload Benefits:**
- ✓ Upload 50+ listings quickly
- ✓ Ideal for importing agency database
- ✓ Less time than manual entry
- ✓ Bulk feature/deactivate later

## Lead & Contact Management {#lead-management}

### Understanding Your Leads

**Types of Leads:**

1. **Active Leads**: Buyers/renters currently interested
   - Have messaged you or sent inquiries
   - In active discussion
   - May convert to transaction

2. **Warm Leads**: Showed interest but not conversing
   - Viewed your listings
   - Added to favorites
   - Haven't reached out yet

3. **Cold Leads**: Initial contact only
   - One message exchange
   - Haven't engaged further
   - Need follow-up

4. **Unqualified Leads**: Not serious buyers
   - Spam or fake inquiries
   - Price fishing
   - Not legitimate prospects

### Lead Organization

**Accessing Your Leads:**

1. Go to **Leads** from dashboard
2. See all incoming inquiries and interested buyers

**Lead Status:**

- **New** (unread)
- **Contacted** (you've responded)
- **Qualified** (verified buyer/renter)
- **In Process** (active negotiation)
- **Converted** (transaction completed)
- **Lost** (no longer interested)

**Lead Filters:**

Filter by:
- Status
- Property (which listing they're interested in)
- Date range
- Buyer/Renter type

### Managing Leads Effectively

**Lead Window:**

When someone contacts you about a property:
1. Their name and contact info
2. Message/inquiry
3. Property they're interested in
4. Viewing history

**Responding to Leads:**

1. Click lead to open conversation
2. Read their inquiry carefully
3. Compose professional response:
   - Answer their specific questions
   - Suggest property strengths related to their needs
   - Offer to schedule viewing
   - Provide availability for calls/viewings
4. Send response
5. They receive notification
6. Status changes to "Contacted"

**Response Time Strategy:**
- ✓ Reply within 1-2 hours for best conversion
- ✓ Set expectations: "I'll have photos for you by tomorrow"
- ✓ Same-day response = better lead quality
- ✗ Delays reduce lead conversion significantly

### Scheduling Property Viewings

**Proposing a Viewing:**

1. In lead conversation or listing, offer multiple time slots:
   - "Available Tuesday at 2 PM or Wednesday at 10 AM"
   - Provide flexibility where possible
2. Include viewing details:
   - Exact address
   - Parking information
   - Entry instructions
   - Your contact number for day-of
3. Send viewing confirmation

**Managing Viewing Appointments:**

1. Go to **Calendar** or **Appointments**
2. See all scheduled viewings
3. Get reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before
4. Email confirmation sent to client automatically

**Preparing for Showings:**

- ✓ Arrive 15 minutes early
- ✓ Ensure property is clean and presentable
- ✓ Open windows for fresh air
- ✓ Turn on all lights
- ✓ Have lockbox or key ready
- ✓ Bring property information sheets
- ✓ Write down client feedback

### Follow-up Strategy

**Automated Reminders:**

Set up follow-up reminders:
1. After first contact: Send market info or similar listings
2. After viewing: Ask for feedback, offer more properties
3. After 3 days: Check in on their decision process
4. After 7 days: Send new matching listings

**Follow-up Best Practices:**

**Day 1** (After Initial Contact):
- Answer questions quickly
- Demonstrate expertise
- Suggest related properties

**Days 1-3** (After Viewing):
- Call/message for feedback
- Share market insights
- Address concerns
- Propose next steps

**Days 3-7**:
- Send matching new listings
- Update on price changes
- Market analysis
- Valuable content (not just sales)

**Days 7+**:
- Check interest level
- Offer alternative properties
- Stay in contact for future needs

## Communication & Follow-up {#communication}

### In-App Messaging

**Accessing Messages:**

1. Click **"Messages"** from navigation
2. See all client conversations
3. Unread messages show badge count

**Message Types:**

- **Lead Inquiries**: Buyers asking about specific properties
- **Negotiation**: Back-and-forth on price/terms
- **Administrative**: Scheduling, documents, logistics
- **Follow-up**: Checking on decision process
- **Post-Sale**: After transaction completed

**Messaging Best Practices:**

- ✓ Be professional and courteous
- ✓ Answer all questions directly
- ✓ Use proper grammar and formatting
- ✓ Include property details when relevant
- ✓ Respond quickly (hours, not days)
- ✓ Suggest next steps
- ✗ Share bank/payment info through chat
- ✗ Accept payments in-app (direct transactions)
- ✗ Be pushy or aggressive
- ✗ Discuss legal advice (refer to lawyer)

### Organizing Conversations

**Archive Conversations:**

After transaction completes:
1. Click three-dot menu
2. Select **"Archive"**
3. Conversation moves to archive but stays searchable
4. Doesn't clutter active inbox

**Save Important Details:**

For reference:
1. Right-click message → **"Pin"** (keeps at top)
2. Or create note in contact details
3. Save key client preferences

**Search Messages:**

1. Use search bar to find messages
2. Search by name, property, content
3. Quickly locate prior conversations

### Professional Communication Tips

**First Contact Response:**

Especially important for lead conversion:
- Acknowledge their inquiry specifically
- Demonstrate knowledge of the property
- Ask qualifying questions
- Offer value immediately
- Use their name (personalization)

**Example Professional Response:**
> "Hi Sarah, Thanks for your interest in the 3-bedroom home at 123 Oak Street! I can see why you'd love it—that lot is perfect for gardening, and the neighborhood has excellent schools which you mentioned interest in. I'd love to schedule a showing at your convenience. Are you available Saturday morning or would Sunday work better? Best, Michael"

**Handling Objections:**

When client expresses concerns:
- Listen and acknowledge
- Don't be defensive
- Provide facts/market data
- Offer solutions
- Know when to refer to other professionals (lender, inspector)

## Performance Analytics {#performance-analytics}

### Accessing Your Analytics

1. Go to **Analytics** from dashboard
2. Choose time period (This Month, 90 Days, Year-to-Date)
3. View comprehensive performance metrics

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

**Listing Performance:**

- **Total Views**: How many people viewed your listings
- **Views per Listing**: Average views per property
- **Favorites**: How many users saved your listings
- **Inquiry Rate**: % of views that resulted in inquiries
- **Conversion Rate**: % of inquiries that led to transactions

**Market Position:**

- **Rank**: Your rank among agents in your area
- **Listings by Agent**: How many listings competitors have
- **Price Competitiveness**: How your prices compare

**Client Engagement:**

- **Response Time**: Average time to respond to inquiries
- **Message Rate**: % of inquiries you respond to
- **Viewing Convert Rate**: % of viewings scheduled

**Sales Activity:**

- **Completed Transactions**: Total sales/rentals closed
- **Commission Earned**: Average per transaction
- **Days on Market**: Average time from listing to sale

### Performance Reports

**Generating Reports:**

1. Go to **Reports** section
2. Available report types:
   - Monthly Performance Upload
   - Quarterly Summary
   - Annual Review
   - Client Activity Report
   - Custom Report

**Customizing Reports:**

1. Select date range
2. Choose metrics to include
3. Select format: PDF, Excel, PowerPoint
4. **"Generate Report"**
5. Download or email report

**Comparative Analysis:**

- **Market Trends**: Compare to local market data
- **Competitor Data**: See how you rank vs other agents
- **Historical**: Compare this month to last month/year

**Sharing Reports:**

1. Generate report
2. Click **"Share"**
3. Enter email addresses
4. Send to clients, management, or team
5. Recipients get PDF summary

### Using Analytics to Improve

**Performance Optimization:**

- **Low Views?** → Improve photo quality, add video, feature listing
- **High Views, Few Inquiries?** → Lower price or improve description
- **Slow Response?** → Set phone notifications, prioritize leads
- **Few Inquiries?** → Market listing more, improve title, target ads

**Client Insights:**

- What properties are they viewing?
- What neighborhoods interest them?
- What price range?
- Use data to suggest better matches

Featured listings appear at the top of search results, getting 3-5x more visibility than regular listings.

**Cost & Duration:**

**Using Lead Scores:**
