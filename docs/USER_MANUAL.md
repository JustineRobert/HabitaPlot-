## Getting Started {#getting-started}

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the **HabitaPlot™ home page**
2. Click the **"Sign Up"** button in the top-right corner
3. Select your user type:
   - **Buyer** - Looking to purchase property
   - **Renter** - Looking to rent property
   - **Seller** - Selling your property
   - **Landlord** - Listing rental properties
   - **Real Estate Agent** - Professional property agent

4. Enter your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password (Must be at least 8 characters with mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols)
   - Confirm Password

5. Read and accept the **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**
6. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox for the verification message from HabitaPlot™
2. Click the **"Verify Email"** link in the email (valid for 24 hours)
3. If the link expired, click **"Resend Verification"** on the login page
4. Your account is now active!

### Step 3: Set Up Your Profile

1. **Log in** to your HabitaPlot™ account
2. Click your **profile icon** (top-right corner)
3. Select **"My Profile"** or **"Account Settings"**
4. Complete your profile information:
   - Add a profile picture (optional but recommended)
   - Write a brief bio (for agents and landlords)
   - Enter your location/city
   - Specify your role/profession
5. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Step 4: Complete KYC Verification (Required)

Know Your Customer (KYC) verification is required to post listings and unlock certain features.

**For Individual Buyers/Renters:**
1. Navigate to **Account Settings** → **KYC Verification**
2. Choose your document type:
   - National ID Card
   - Passport
   - Driver's License
3. Upload clear images (front and back)
4. Submit for review
5. Verification typically completes within 24-48 hours
6. You'll receive an email notification when approved

**For Real Estate Agents/Businesses:**
1. Navigate to **Account Settings** → **Business Verification**
2. Upload:
   - Business Registration Certificate
   - Tax ID/Registration Number
   - Agent License (if applicable)
3. Submit for review
4. Verification typically completes within 2-3 business days

✓ **Once approved, you can:**
- Post unlimited listings (based on subscription)
- Contact other verified users
- Feature your listings
- Access advanced analytics

## Browsing Listings {#browsing-listings}

### Basic Search

1. From the **home page**, you'll see the main search bar
2. Enter your search criteria:
   - **Location**: Enter city, neighborhood, district, or address
   - **Property Type**: Select from dropdown (Apartment, House, Commercial, Land, etc.)
   - **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum price
   - **Other Details**: Bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage

3. Click **"Search"** or press Enter
4. Results will display instantly

### Advanced Filtering

After running a search, use the **filter panel** (left sidebar) to refine results:

**Property Filters:**
- Property Type (Apartment, House, Condo, Townhouse, etc.)
- Available Action (Sell, Rent, Lease)
- District / Administrative Area
- Bedrooms (1+, 2+, 3+, 4+)
- Bathrooms (1+, 2+, 3+)
- Square Footage (range slider)
- Price Range (range slider)

**Amenities:**
- Parking
- Swimming Pool
- Gym/Fitness
- Garden
- Furnished/Unfurnished
- Air Conditioning
- Heating

**Additional Filters:**
- Listing Type (Private Seller, Real Estate Agent)
- Featured Listings (Yes/No)
- Newly Listed (Last 7 days, Last 30 days)
- Verified Seller Only
- Negotiable Price
- Flexible Terms

### Sorting Options

Click the **"Sort"** dropdown to arrange listings by:
- Newest First (default)
- Most Recent
- Price: Low to High
- Price: High to Low
- Distance (closest to farthest)
- Most Viewed
- Most Favorited
- Best Reviewed

### Saving Your Search

1. After customizing filters, click **"Save This Search"**
2. Give your search a name (e.g., "2BHK Apartments Near Downtown")
3. Choose notification preferences:
   - No notifications
   - Daily digest
   - Weekly digest
   - Instant notifications
4. Click **"Save Search"**
5. View saved searches under **"My Searches"** in your dashboard

### Viewing Property Details

1. Click on any property card to view the **full listing page**
2. You'll see:
   - **Photo Gallery**: Swipe through high-quality images
   - **Property Details**: Location, price, property type, condition, size
   - **Agent/Seller Info**: Contact details and verification status
   - **Description**: Detailed property description
   - **Amenities**: List of included features
   - **Price Breakdown**: (For rentals: rent, security deposit, utility costs)
   - **Neighborhood Info**: Schools, transit, shopping
   - **Map View**: Exact property location
   - **User Reviews**: Ratings and comments from previous renters/buyers
   - **Action Buttons**: Add to Favorites, Contact Agent, Schedule Viewing, Share

### Map View

1. Click the **"Map View"** tab (if available)
2. See all properties in the area displayed on an interactive map
3. Click on individual property pins for quick preview
4. Zoom in/out and drag to explore different areas
5. Use the filter panel on the left to refine the map results

## Contacting Sellers & Agents {#contacting}

### Sending Your First Message

1. From a property listing, click **"Contact Seller"** or **"Contact Agent"**
2. A **messaging popup** or **new page** will open
3. Type your message:
   - Ask about specific features
   - Request more photos
   - Ask to schedule a viewing
   - Inquire about terms and conditions
4. Click **"Send"**
5. The seller/agent will receive your message within minutes

### In-App Messaging

All communication happens through HabitaPlot™'s secure messaging system:

**Benefits:**
- ✓ Messages are encrypted and secure
- ✓ No need to share personal contact info initially
- ✓ Can block or report abusive users
- ✓ Conversation history is saved
- ✓ Get notifications when a new message arrives

**Accessing Your Messages:**
1. Click the **"Messages"** icon (top navigation)
2. View all conversations in the **inbox**
3. Click any conversation to view message history
4. Type and send replies

### Scheduling Property Viewings

1. In the property listing, click **"Schedule Viewing"** or **"Book Appointment"**
2. Select your preferred date and time from available slots
3. Enter any special requests (e.g., "I have a dog", "Please show parking spots")
4. Click **"Request Appointment"**
5. The agent will confirm or propose alternative times
6. Set a **reminder** for the viewing time
7. The day of viewing, the agent will send you the exact address/location details

### Reporting & Safety

If you receive suspicious messages or feel unsafe:

1. Click the **three-dot menu** on the problematic message
2. Select **"Report Message"** or **"Report User"**
3. Choose the reason:
   - Spam or Scam
   - Harassment or Abuse
   - Inappropriate Content
   - Other
4. Include a brief explanation
5. Click **"Submit Report"**
6. Our safety team will review within 24 hours

**Never share:**
- Bank account information
- Credit card details
- Social Security numbers
- Passwords

## Posting Your Own Listings {#posting-listings}

*This section applies if you're a Seller, Landlord, or Real Estate Agent.*

### Step 1: Prepare Your Information

Before you start, gather:
- High-quality photos/videos (10+ recommended)
- Property address and details
- Price or rental rate
- List of amenities
- Property condition details
- Any additional selling points

### Step 2: Start Creating a Listing

1. Click **"Post Listing"** or **"Create Listing"** (top navigation or dashboard)
2. Select your **property type**:
   - House
   - Apartment
   - Condo
   - Commercial
   - Land
   - Other
3. Select the **action type**:
   - For Sale
   - For Rent
   - For Lease

### Step 3: Enter Property Details

**Location Information:**
1. Enter the complete address
2. The map will auto-populate the exact location
3. Adjust the pin if needed for accuracy

**Property Information:**
1. Property Type (House, Apartment, etc.)
2. Total Area (in sq ft or sq meters)
3. Number of Bedrooms
4. Number of Bathrooms
5. Year Built
6. Property Condition (Excellent, Good, Fair, Needs Renovation)
7. Furnishing (Furnished, Unfurnished, Semi-furnished)

**Pricing:**
- **For Sales**: Enter the asking price
- **For Rentals**: 
  - Monthly rent
  - Security deposit
  - Any additional fees (parking, utilities, etc.)
  - Lease term (6 months, 1 year, negotiable)

### Step 4: Add Description & Amenities

**Property Description:**
1. Write a compelling description (200-1000 characters)
2. Highlight key features:
   - Architectural style
   - Recent renovations
   - Unique selling points
   - Parking facilities
   - Proximity to landmarks

**Select Amenities:**
Check all applicable amenities:
- [ ] Air Conditioning
- [ ] Heating System
- [ ] Parking (1, 2, 3+ spots)
- [ ] Garage
- [ ] Garden/Outdoor Space
- [ ] Balcony/Patio
- [ ] Swimming Pool
- [ ] Gym/Fitness Center
- [ ] Security/Gated Community
- [ ] Elevator
- [ ] Wheelchair Accessible
- [ ] Pet-Friendly
- [ ] Furnished Kitchen
- [ ] Laundry
- [ ] Internet/Cable Ready

### Step 5: Upload Photos & Video

**Photo Upload:**
1. Click **"Upload Photos"**
2. Select 10-20 high-quality images
3. Arrange them in order (front, bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living areas, amenities)
4. Add captions to each photo (optional)
5. Set your cover image (first photo shown in searches)

**Photo Tips:**
- ✓ Use natural lighting during daytime
- ✓ Keep photos horizontal (landscape)
- ✓ Include wide-angle shots of main rooms
- ✓ Show condition details
- ✓ Include outdoor/garden photos
- ✓ Avoid cluttered backgrounds
- ✗ Don't use blurry or low-resolution images

**Video Tour (Premium Feature):**
1. Click **"Upload Video"**
2. Select a video file (MP4, MOV, WebM)
3. Maximum 5 minutes
4. Shows full property walkthrough

### Step 6: Set Contact & Availability

**Contact Information:**
1. Phone Number (to be contacted about this property)
2. Email Address
3. Best time to contact
4. Languages spoken

**Availability:**
1. Are you available for viewings immediately?
2. Set specific viewing hours
3. Schedule any unavailable dates/times

### Step 7: Preview & Publish

1. Click **"Preview Listing"** to see how it will appear to buyers
2. Check all details are correct
3. Verify photos display properly
4. Read through the description
5. Click **"Publish Listing"** to make it live

**Your listing is now visible to:**
- All registered buyers
- Real estate professionals
- Search engines (if indexing enabled)

### Step 8: Manage Your Listing

After publishing, you can:

**View Performance:**
- Go to **Dashboard** → **"My Listings"**
- See views, favorites, contact requests
- Track inquiry trends

**Edit Your Listing:**
1. Click **"Edit"** on your listing
2. Modify any details, photos, or price
3. Save changes (updates appear immediately)

**Feature Your Listing:**
1. Click **"Make Featured"** (requires subscription upgrade)
2. Select duration (7 days, 30 days)
3. Complete payment
4. Your listing will appear at top of search results

**Deactivate/Relist Listing:**
1. Click **"Deactivate"** to temporarily hide
2. Click **"Relist"** to reactivate
3. Or **"Delete"** to permanently remove

## Security & Privacy {#security}

### Keeping Your Account Secure

**Password Best Practices:**
- Use passwords with at least 8 characters
- Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Don't share your password with anyone
- Change your password every 90 days
- Never set password as just a simple word or date

**Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):**
1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**
3. Choose authentication method:
   - **Authenticator App** (Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator)
   - **SMS Text Message**
4. Follow the setup wizard
5. Save backup codes in a safe place
6. Once enabled, you'll need to enter a code from your app/phone when logging in

**Session Management:**
1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Active Sessions**
2. View all devices currently logged into your account
3. See device type, location, and last active time
4. Click **"Sign Out"** on any device to end that session
5. If you don't recognize a session, click **"Sign Out Everywhere"** and reset your password

### Privacy Controls

**What Information You Share:**
1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Control what's visible:
   - [ ] Show my phone number to agents
   - [ ] Show my email to contacted users
   - [ ] Show my profile to buyers/sellers
   - [ ] Show my listings publicly
   - [ ] Allow search engines to find my profile
3. Save preferences

**Blocking Users:**
1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Blocked Users**
2. Click **"Block User"**
3. Enter the user's name or email
4. Select reason (optional)
5. The user cannot message you or see your listings
6. To unblock: Click **"Unblock"** next to their name

**Data Access & Export:**
1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Data & Privacy**
2. Click **"Request My Data"**
3. Receive a downloadable file containing all your data (within 30 days)
4. Leave your email and we'll send it when ready

**Delete Your Data:**
1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Delete Account**
2. Type "DELETE" to confirm
3. Choose what to delete:
   - [ ] Delete all my personal data
   - [ ] Keep my listings (deactivated)
   - [ ] Delete everything
4. Confirm deletion
5. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

### Recognizing Scams

**Common Scams to Watch For:**

❌ **Overly Low Prices**
- If a property is significantly cheaper than market rate, it may be a scam
- Verify the property exists and is listed by legitimate agent

❌ **Requests for Money Before Viewing**
- Legitimate sellers/agents never ask for money upfront
- Not even for "holding fee" or "deposit" before seeing the property

❌ **Poor Grammar/Communication**
- Scammers often have poor English and suspicious messages
- Verify through video call before meeting

❌ **Pressure to Act Quickly**
- "Only available today!"
- "Another buyer is interested"
- Legitimate sales have time for due diligence

❌ **Missing Proper Documentation**
- Ask to verify the seller's ID
- Request proof of ownership or agency license
- Get everything in writing

**If You Suspect a Scam:**
1. Do NOT send any money
2. Do NOT share personal/financial info
3. Click **"Report Listing"** or **"Report User"** on HabitaPlot™
4. Contact our support team immediately
5. Save all communication evidence

## Frequently Asked Questions {#faq}

### Account & Registration

**Q: How do I create an account?**  
A: Click "Sign Up" on the home page, choose your user type, enter your email and password, verify your email, and complete your profile.

**Q: Do I need to verify my email?**  
A: Yes, email verification is required within 24 hours to activate your account.

**Q: What is KYC verification and why do I need it?**  
A: KYC (Know Your Customer) is an identity verification process required to post listings and access certain features. Upload a valid government ID and we'll verify within 24-48 hours.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: Yes, go to Account Settings → Personal Information and update your email. We'll send a verification link to your new email.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**  
A: No, one person can only have one account. Multiple accounts may result in permanent suspension.

### Browsing & Searching

**Q: How many listings can I search at once?**  
A: You can view search results with pagination. Most pages show 10-20 listings per page.

**Q: Can I save searches to get alerts?**  
A: Yes, click "Save This Search" after customizing filters. Set your alert frequency (instant, daily, weekly).

**Q: What does "Featured" mean?**  
A: Featured listings appear at the top of search results and are promoted for 7-30 days. Sellers can upgrade their listings for a fee.

**Q: Can I filter by multiple criteria?**  
A: Yes, use the Advanced Filters panel on the left to combine multiple filters (price, location, bedrooms, amenities, etc.).

**Q: How can I see a property on a map?**  
A: Click "Map View" on the search results to see all properties displayed on an interactive map.

### Posting Listings

**Q: How many listings can I post?**  
A: Free users can post 1 listing. Premium members get 5, Featured tier gets 20, and Enterprise members get unlimited.

**Q: How much does it cost to post a listing?**  
A: Basic listing is free for all members. Premium features (featured, promoted) require subscription upgrades.

**Q: What photo requirements do listings have?**  
A: We recommend 10-20 high-quality photos in landscape orientation. Files should be under 10MB each and in common formats (JPG, PNG).

**Q: How long do listings stay active?**  
A: Listings remain active indefinitely until you deactivate or delete them.

**Q: Can I edit a listing after publishing?**  
A: Yes, go to "My Listings" and click "Edit" on any listing to update details, photos, or price. Changes take effect immediately.

**Q: Can I feature a listing later?**  
A: Yes, from "My Listings" click "Feature" or "Make Featured" on any active listing.

### Contacting & Communication

**Q: How do I contact a seller or agent?**  
A: Click "Contact Seller" or "Contact Agent" from the property listing to send a message through our secure platform.

**Q: Are my messages private?**  
A: Yes, all messages are encrypted and only visible to you and the recipient.

**Q: What if someone harasses me?**  
A: Click the three-dot menu on the message and select "Report User." Our support team reviews all reports.

**Q: Can I call sellers directly?**  
A: You can request their phone number after initial messaging, but our platform doesn't provide direct calling.

**Q: How long does it take to get a response?**  
A: Most sellers/agents respond within a few hours. Premium subscribers receive faster support.

### Payments & Subscriptions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), digital wallets (PayPal, Apple Pay), bank transfers, and local Ugandan mobile money options such as MTN MoMo and Airtel Money (when enabled for your region).

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**  
A: Yes, all payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways. We never store full credit card numbers.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?**

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### "Email Verification Link Expired"

**Problem:** The verification link you received was older than 24 hours.

**Solution:**
1. Log in with your email and password
2. You should see a banner about unverified email
3. Click "Resend Verification Email"
4. Check your inbox (and spam folder) within 5 minutes
5. Click the new verification link immediately

---

#### "I Can't Log In"

**Problem:** You're receiving an error when trying to log in.

**Solutions:**
1. First, verify you're using the correct email and password
2. Try **"Forgot Password"** to reset your password
3. Check if your email is verified
4. Clear your browser cookies and cache, then try again
5. Try a different browser
6. Contact support if you still can't log in

---

#### "Email Address Already Exists"

**Problem**: You're getting an error that this email is already registered.

**Solutions:**
1. You might already have an account - try logging in with that email
2. If you forgot which account it is, use "Forgot Password"
3. Contact support with proof of identity if you believe it's an error

---

#### "Photos Won't Upload"

**Problem:** Your photos are failing to upload to a listing.

**Solutions:**
1. Check file size (should be under 10 MB each)
2. Verify file format (JPG, PNG, WebP are supported)
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try uploading one photo at a time
5. Try a different browser
6. Resize the image (too large dimensions may fail)

---

#### "I Can't See My Listing"

**Problem:** Your listing is not appearing in search results.

**Solutions:**
1. Verify the listing status shows "Active" in "My Listings"
2. If status is "Pending," wait 24-48 hours for admin review
3. Double-check your filters - you might be filtering it out
4. Search by address directly to find it
5. Try incognito mode to see your actual listing
6. If it says "Rejected," review the reason and resubmit with corrections

---

#### "I'm Not Receiving Messages"

**Problem:** You're not getting notified of new messages.

**Solutions:**
1. Go to Account Settings → Preferences
2. Verify "Email Notifications" is enabled for messages
3. Check your spam/junk folder for notification emails
4. Check if the conversation is "muted"
5. Log into HabitaPlot™ directly to see if messages are there
6. Make sure notifications are enabled in your browser settings

---

#### "My Subscription Charge Didn't Go Through"

**Problem:** Payment failed but you're not sure why.

**Solutions:**
1. Check your bank/card to see if there's a pending or failed transaction
2. Try adding a different payment method
3. Ensure your card expiration date and CVV are correct
4. Try a different browser or clear browser cache
5. Contact your bank to verify the card is active
6. Check if there are any fraud blocks

---

#### "I See a Payment but No Subscription Upgrade"

**Problem:** Your payment went through but your subscription didn't upgrade.

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page or log out and back in
2. Check your email for a confirmation receipt
3. It may take a few minutes to process - check again in 5-10 minutes
4. Go to Account Settings → Subscription to verify current plan
5. If still showing as not upgraded, contact support with your order number

---

#### "I Need to Recover a Deleted Listing"

**Problem:** You accidentally deleted a listing and want it back.

**Solutions:**
1. Deleted listings are gone permanently after confirmation
2. If it was very recent, contact support immediately - they may be able to recover it
3. Recreate the listing with the same information
4. Save important listing details (descriptions, photos) before deleting in the future

---

#### "The Map Is Not Loading"

**Problem:** The map view shows a blank or error state.

**Solutions:**
1. Zoom out on the map
2. Check if the address is valid (maps can't load invalid addresses)
3. Refresh the page
4. Try a different browser
5. Disable browser extensions (some block maps)
6. Check your internet connection

---

#### "I Can't Find a Listing I Saw Before"

**Problem:** A property listing has disappeared.

**Solutions:**
1. The listing may have been sold/rented - the seller deactivated it
2. Try searching for similar properties in the area
3. Check "My Favorites" if you saved it
4. Check your saved searches
5. Note the address and contact the agent directly
