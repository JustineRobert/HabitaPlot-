# 90-Day Launch Roadmap

## Goal
Launch HabitaPlot™ in Uganda with district-aware listings, local mobile money payments, and an early adopter agent network.

## Phase 1: Days 1-30 — Product Completion

### Objectives
- Finalize district localization and listing workflows.
- Complete MTN MoMo and Airtel Money payment integration.
- Prepare primary wireframes and early marketing collateral.

### Key Tasks
- Finish backend support for `district` field and search filters.
- Finish frontend search UI and listing detail enhancements.
- Build payment initiation and verification flows.
- Draft compliance, terms, and privacy documentation.
- Define pitch deck and launch messaging.
- Set up analytics, logging, and basic operational dashboards.

### Deliverables
- Working MVP with listing creation and district search.
- Mobile money checkout proof-of-concept.
- Figma-ready wireframes and pitch deck outline.
- Legal draft documentation.

## Phase 2: Days 31-60 — Market Preparation

### Objectives
- Onboard initial agents and early listings.
- Validate payment flows with pilot customers.
- Execute initial marketing for Kampala and Central Region.

### Key Tasks
- Recruit and onboard 10-15 local agents.
- Publish sample listings across key districts.
- Run targeted digital campaigns and partner outreach.
- Gather user feedback from buyers and sellers.
- Refine UX based on pilot testing.
- Confirm mobile money settlement and transaction tracking.

### Deliverables
- Active agent network and seed listings.
- Customer feedback report.
- Updated launch messaging and marketing assets.
- Payment operations checklist.

## Phase 3: Days 61-90 — Launch & Growth

### Objectives
- Execute public launch in top Uganda districts.
- Scale listing volume and user acquisition.
- Monitor performance, support, and retention.

### Key Tasks
- Launch press and social campaigns for Kampala, Wakiso, Mukono.
- Offer promotional premium listing credits.
- Optimize search relevance for district queries.
- Provide agent support and onboarding resources.
- Track KPIs: listings, searches, signups, payments completed.
- Address issues quickly with a dedicated launch team.

### Deliverables
- Public launch announcement and marketing campaign.
- Launch analytics dashboard with daily metrics.
- Initial retention and conversion analysis.
- Bug and product improvement backlog.

## Risks & Mitigations
- Payment provider delays
  - Mitigation: support both MTN MoMo and Airtel Money; fallback to card checkout.
- Low listing density
  - Mitigation: onboard agents with incentive offers and featured listing discounts.
- Regulatory changes
  - Mitigation: maintain compliance review and local counsel input.

## Success Metrics
- 100+ active listings in target districts
- 1,000+ monthly searches
- 50+ completed mobile money transactions
- 80%+ agent onboarding satisfaction
- Positive launch feedback from first buyer cohort
